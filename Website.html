Welcome To

K.S. School Of Business Management And Information Technology (KSSBMIT)

  • K. S. School of Business Management is one of the pioneer institutes offering Integrated MBA and MSc (Computer Applications & Information Technology) in India. Established under the aegis of Gujarat University, KSSBM started with a five-year integrated program in MBA in 1993, followed by a five-year integrated program in MSc (CA&IT) in 1998. Both programs have grown in size and stature, attracting Gujarat state's young budding students after their 12th standard from all walks of life.
  • Our mission at the school is "Igniting young minds to grow in terms of knowledge and wisdom and stand up high in the corporate world." With the support of a syllabus updated to the latest changes in subject and content on the lines of the CBCS pattern, project study, and field study, students are nurtured and groomed over five years to meet industry expectations. And this is made possible by the unconditional support of highly qualified faculty members who are endowed with "committed to contribute".
  • The institute is located in the rich flora and fauna environment of the Gujarat University campus. The institute is equipped with state-of-the-art facilities like LCD projectors, 3 computer laboratories with the latest version of hardware and software, and a library with a rich collection of books, journals, and other learning aids.
  • A full-fledged Placement Cell, under the guidance of a faculty placement coordinator, is operational for each of the courses separately. The placement heads of the respective courses groom the students to face companies in interviews and facilitate students' smooth transition from college to corporate. Over the years, our Alumni are spread across national and international borders.
  • To harness the skills and personality of students, the school constantly engages students in co-curricular and extracurricular activities. Academic and cultural events and competitions are held regularly, giving the students the opportunity to showcase their talents.
We Provide
  • MBA: Getting an MBA degree is a wise way to shape your career. MBA graduates are generally sought after by employers because they possess managerial skills that are essential for a company. This will help you withstand any competitive environment, thereby broadening your career opportunities.
  • MSC.IT: Master of Science in Information Technology (M.Sc(IT)) is a postgraduate program designed to provide in-depth knowledge and skills in the field of information technology. This advanced degree focuses on the study of computer science, software engineering, systems analysis, data management, and networking.
